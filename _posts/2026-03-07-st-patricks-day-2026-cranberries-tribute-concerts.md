St. Patrick's Day 2026: Cranberries tribute concerts between Italy and the United Kingdom ☘️

<!--more-->

<!-- more -->

St. Patrick's Day 2026 is approaching and with it one of the most beloved holidays dedicated to Irish culture returns. Between shamrocks, Guinness and live music, the best way to celebrate Ireland is by singing the great hits of the Cranberries, one of the most iconic bands on the Irish rock scene.

For the occasion, a special mini tour for St. Patrick's Day 2026 will begin, with concerts between Italy and the United Kingdom, dedicated to the music of the Cranberries and the extraordinary voice of Dolores O'Riordan.

Here are all the dates where you can experience the atmosphere of St. Patrick's Day with live music.

## Cranberries acoustic concert in Busto Arsizio

The St. Patrick's Day 2026 tour will begin with a special acoustic evening in Lombardy, perfect to warm up the atmosphere before the official celebrations.

📍 The Millennium Irish Pub – Busto Arsizio (VA)</br>
🕘 8.30pm</br>
🎸 An acoustic journey through Dolores's most beloved melodies.

During the concert, acoustic versions of the Cranberries' most beloved songs will be performed, in a musical journey that passes through the symbolic songs of the Irish band.</br>
An ideal event for those who love live music in Irish-style pubs and want to get into the St. Patrick's spirit.

## St. Patrick's Day in England: three concerts between Liverpool, Manchester and Chester

After the Italian date, the tour will move to the United Kingdom, with three concerts dedicated to the music of the Cranberries.

## Concert in Liverpool – Invisible Wind Factory

📍 Liverpool</br>
🕘 Saturday at 9pm

Liverpool is a symbolic city of international music. In this unique location we will bring to the stage a tribute to the Cranberries at the heart of the British music scene.

## Concert in Manchester – Freight Island

📍 Manchester</br>
🕑 Sunday 2pm

Manchester will host an afternoon live show dedicated to the Cranberries' great classics, in one of the most dynamic spaces dedicated to music and live events.

## Concert in Chester – Alexander’s Live

📍 Chester</br>
🕘 Sunday 9pm

The British weekend will end with an evening concert in one of the historic clubs of English live music.

## ☘️ St. Patrick's Day 2026 in Milan: concert at the Hard Rock Cafe ☘️

The grand finale of St. Patrick's Day 2026 will be held on March 17, the official day of the Irish holiday.

📍 Hard Rock Cafe – Milan (Via Dante)</br>
📅 March 17, 2026</br>
🕘 9pm

A special evening to celebrate St. Patrick's Day in Milan, with live music, Irish atmosphere and the Cranberries' great hits to sing together.

If you are looking for what to do in Milan for St. Patrick's Day, this concert will be the perfect opportunity to experience the celebration of rock, beer and Irish spirit.
