The Queen of Limerick finally arrives in Ireland for an evening that promises to be unforgettable.

<!--more-->

On 31 May 2026 at 8pm, the show will be staged at the Royal Theater in Castlebar, one of the country's leading theatres.
A special event that will bring music, dance and emotion to the stage in an intense story dedicated to Dolores O'Riordan, symbolic voice of the Cranberries.
## An Irish rock recital between music and dance
The Queen of Limerick is much more than just a tribute. It is an Irish rock recital capable of combining concert, story and choreography in an engaging and original show.
The story comes to life through an intense and authentic flow of memory, in which the music of the Cranberries is intertwined with the expressive power of Irish dance.
Tradition and modernity come together in a unique performance, capable of exciting the audience from start to finish.
## A tribute to Dolores O'Riordan and Ireland
On stage, together with **<ahref="https://www.gensdys.it/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Gens D'ys</a>**, **<ahref="https://andrearock.it/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Andrea Rock</a>** and **<ahref="https://www.instagram.com/deborah_dnasty/"target="_blank" rel="noopener">Deborah Pini</a>**, a profound celebration of Irish music and culture will be staged.
Each scene tells the soul of Ireland: its history, its contradictions and its extraordinary beauty.
The rock sounds of The Cranberries blend with the rhythms of traditional dances, creating a show that is not limited to being seen, but is experienced.
It is a sincere tribute to a voice that marked an era and to a land that continues to inspire emotions.
## The Royal Theater in Castlebar: a perfect setting
The **<a href="https://tfroyal.ie/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Royal Theater Castlebar</a>** is the ideal location for this event.
One of the most prestigious stages in Ireland, capable of amplifying every note, every dance step and every emotion.
Bringing The Queen of Limerick right here has a profound meaning.
It's like bringing this story home.
## A special journey to experience together
For us this show also represents a dream come true.
Leaving for Ireland with a company of extraordinary dancers and with one of the most loved speakers in Italy will be a unique experience.
We can't wait to share this adventure with you.

**See you in Castlebar.** 💚
