In the band there is a rule, dictated by Cristina — our Liz Kry, voice of the Cranberries tribute.

<!--more-->

Under concert date, nothing that can break. Ankles, fingers, vocal cords: they are respected.

For years he applied it with the rigor of someone who knows that a season is played on a ligament. Then she went to the mountains. Paths, rocks, differences in altitude. She came back intact.

The laundry gave her away.

A puddle under the clothes on the line. The most prudent of us, injured at the most innocuous point of the day. Nothing serious. But the story now belongs to us, and we will tell it at every soundcheck.

Lizberries live at the Irish style wedding

Meanwhile, on Tuesday 16 June, something new: we are playing at an Irish-style wedding for the first time. For a band that lives on Cranberries songs, no invitation sounds more right.

Ireland is our point of origin and our return: the land of Dolores, of the melodies that raised us. Bringing her on the most important day of two people is not an engagement. It's closing a circle.

Strictly green dress code. Cristina will be there, standing and speaking. He has already inspected the floor.
