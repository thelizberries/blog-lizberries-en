## Important statement – The Queen of Limerick

It is with regret that we have to inform you

<!--more-->

that, for organizational reasons beyond our control, the show *The Queen of Limerick* scheduled at the Royal Theater in Castlebar will not be able to take place.<br>
This is news that saddens us greatly, because this project represents something truly special for us.<br>
In recent months we have worked with great passion to build a show into which we have put everything: time, energy, emotions and above all our love for the music of <span>The Cranberries</span> and for the memory of <span>Dolores O'Riordan</span>.

## A project born with the heart

*The Queen of Limerick* was not just a show.<br>
For us it means creating a special moment to share with all those who continue to love the music of the Cranberries and the artistic legacy of Dolores O'Riordan.<br>
For this reason we sincerely regret not being able to bring it on stage at this moment, especially thinking of those who were already waiting for us and supporting us with enthusiasm.

## It's not goodbye

However, we want to reassure everyone on an important point: this is not a definitive cancellation.<br>
We're already working to find the right way to bring this show to you anyway, and new updates will be coming soon.<br>
The *The Queen of Limerick* project continues, and the desire to share this journey with you remains as strong as ever.

## See you in Ireland in September

One thing remains absolutely confirmed: in September we will see each other in Ireland for the tour organized in memory of <span>Dolores O'Riordan</span>'s birthday.<br>
It is an event that we care about very much and that we can't wait to experience together with all of you.<br>
It will be a special moment to once again celebrate the music, memories and emotions that continue to unite Cranberries fans around the world.

## Thank you for your support

We really want to thank everyone for the support, affection and trust that you continue to show us every day.<br>
Your support means a lot to us and gives us even more strength to continue this journey.<br>
<br>
Thank you very much to everyone.<br>
See you very soon.

**The Queen of Limerick Team**
