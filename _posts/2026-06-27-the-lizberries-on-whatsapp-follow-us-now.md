There’s a more direct way to stay in touch. We’re the ones who started it.

We’re not the kind of band that likes to make a fuss just for the sake of it. Since 2010, having formed in the heart of Milan, we’ve been bringing the music of The Cranberries to stages across Europe—from Alcatraz in Milan to Dolan’s in Limerick, the very same venues where The Cranberries played. But when we have something to say—a show date, some news, something that really matters—we want it to reach our true fans, unfiltered and without algorithms deciding for us.

The official Lizberries WhatsApp channel is now live.

## What You’ll Find on the Channel

No spam. No filler content. Just what matters:

- **New live dates** in Italy and abroad: from Milanese pubs to European festivals
- Updates on the **2026 Irish Tour**—Galway, Cork, Limerick, Navan—and every stop on the tour
- Behind-the-scenes glimpses, rehearsals, and stories that don’t make it onto our official social media
- Once-in-a-lifetime moments — that we don’t want you to miss

We chose WhatsApp because it’s direct, personal, and no-nonsense. Just like The Cranberries’ Irish rock sound: straight to the heart, no frills.

## A tribute tour traveling across Europe

These years on stage—in Italy, Ireland, France, Switzerland, Romania, Germany, and the United Kingdom—have taught us one thing: true connection isn’t built on numbers; it’s built on people. With those who come to our concerts, those who listen to *Zombie* or *Dreams* on their headphones at three in the morning, and those who understand the meaning behind Dolores O’Riordan’s words.

The WhatsApp channel is the fastest way to stay up to date—even before news hits social media.

If you’re one of those people, this channel is for you.

A big thank you to those who’ve been following us since before we even had a name on a flyer—and to those who joined later, with the same passion. Keep supporting us to keep The Cranberries’ music alive!
