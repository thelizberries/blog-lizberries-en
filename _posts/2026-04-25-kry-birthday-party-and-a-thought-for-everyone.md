## Liz Kry's birthday on Voice Day
For Liz Kry, April 16th is not a date like any other.

<!--more-->

The voice of The Lizberries celebrates its birthday on Voice Day, a symbolic intersection that says a lot about the link between artistic identity, personal path and stage.
After an intense day of work that ended at 7.30pm, the evening turned into a surprise organized by the band. Dinner, toasts and laughter marked an authentic moment, built over the years between concerts and trips together.

## Best wishes live on Virgin Radio
The birthday also arrived on the radio.
Live on Virgin Radio Italy, during Andrea Rock and Melissa Greta's program, Virgin Generation, a question was asked about Voice Day. The answer was immediate: Dolores O'Riordan.
Not a random quote, but an artistic root that continues to influence every Lizberries performance.
Immediately afterwards came a dedication to herself. A simple but significant gesture.

## The Queen of Limerick in Castlebar
After the birthday, attention shifts to the next appointment.
At the end of May, exactly the 31st, the band will be in Ireland with the recital The Queen of Limerick. The show will be staged at the Royal Theater Castlebar.
This is a recital dedicated to Dolores O'Riordan, which combines music, story and dance.
Bringing it to Ireland has a strong symbolic value. It means bringing this music back to the place where it was born.
