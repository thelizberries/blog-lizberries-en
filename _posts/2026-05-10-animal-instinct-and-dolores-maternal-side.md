THE MEANING OF “ANIMAL INSTINCT”
The song “Animal Instinct” describes maternal instinct as an animal instinct.

<!--more-->

It's about a mother fighting to protect her children
“So take my hands and come with me/we will change reality/so take my hands and we will pray/they won't to take you away”
(So take my hands and come with me/we'll change reality/so take my hands and let's pray/they won't take you away)
But “Animal Instinct” also talks about the especially emotional difficulties that motherhood entails
“Suddenly I was feeling depressed/I was utterly and totally stressed/Do you know you make me cry/Do you know you make me die”
(Suddenly I felt depressed/I was completely and utterly stressed/You know you made me cry/You know you made me die)

HOW ANIMAL INSTINCT WAS BORN
“Animal Instinct” originates from a very specific moment: Dolores was at the supermarket with her son who was throwing a tantrum and a woman suggested she slap him.
She returned home upset and wrote the refrain "Animal instinct, animal instinct to me": the maternal instinct that rebels to protect her little one against the world.

THE VIDEO OF “ANIMAL INSTINCT”
“Animal Instinct” is a very intense and touching video: it is the story of a mother who manages to escape together with her children from the social services center which, it turns out, had taken them away from her.
The three are in a state of total poverty, even sleeping in the car, eating at a fast food restaurant and then leaving without paying and stopping to hitchhike when the car breaks down.
However, the joy of being together and their rediscovered bond manages to make them find happiness in small things like wandering aimlessly or playing with soap bubbles in the middle of a meadow.

DOLORES O' RIODAN AND MOTHERHOOD
Dolores had three children, Taylor, Molly and Dakota.
For her, motherhood was a gift and she considered it the center of her life.
While continuing to work, even during a pregnancy, she always put her family first. Being a mother, she said, is even more satisfying than a career.
Having suffered from emotional disorders, one can imagine the difficulties she had to face but which never really stopped her.
After all, being a mother is also this: a strong love capable of going beyond problems!
